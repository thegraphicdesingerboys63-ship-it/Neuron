Terms of Service

Welcome to Neuron. Before you continue, please read and accept our Terms of Service.

1. Eligibility

You must be at least 13 years old to use Neuron. Users under 18 are encouraged to enable Parental Controls in Settings.

2. Prohibited Content

You agree not to post, share, or transmit:

  • Any content that sexually exploits or targets minors (CSAM)
  • Personal information of others without consent (doxxing)
  • Credible threats of violence against any person or group
  • Hate speech targeting protected characteristics
  • Spam, phishing, or malware
  • Illegal content of any kind

3. AI Moderation

Neuron uses automated AI moderation to detect and remove harmful content. Violations may result in immediate account suspension without warning. Content involving minors will result in permanent bans and may be reported to authorities.

4. Privacy

Messages are stored to provide the service. Administrators can view flagged content. Do not share sensitive personal information in chats.

5. Enforcement

We reserve the right to remove any content and suspend any account at any time for violations of these terms.

6. Parental Controls

If you are a parent or guardian of a minor using this service, you may enable Parental Controls in the user Settings. This activates content filtering and restricts messaging to friends only.
