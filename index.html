Terms of Service

Welcome to Neuron. Please read and accept our Terms of Service to continue.

1. Eligibility

You must be at least 13 years old to use Neuron. Users under 18 are encouraged to enable Parental Controls.

2. Prohibited Content

You agree not to post:

  • Any content that sexually exploits or targets minors (CSAM)
  • Personal information of others without consent (doxxing)
  • Credible threats of violence
  • Hate speech targeting protected characteristics
  • Spam, phishing, or malware
  • Illegal content of any kind

3. AI Moderation

Neuron uses automated moderation. Critical violations result in immediate bans.

4. Privacy

Messages are stored to provide the service. Admins can view flagged content.

5. Enforcement

We reserve the right to remove content and suspend accounts at any time.
